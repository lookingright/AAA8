Our Story


We started our business at early 90s as a retail fruit store with just one outlets. In late 90s, we had changed our business strategy to become a wholesaler and distributor.
Due to our dedication to deliver quality products and services, we are now one of the largest fresh fruit distributors in Hong Kong, Macau, Europe, and Australia;
and to service customers including airline catering, foodservices, 5 stars hotels, bakeries and major supermarket chain stores.
In 2007, we had expanded into our new 6 floor building premises, located at Chai Wan. The expansion gives easier access to Hong Kong sea port and Hong Kong airport.
Each floor is equipped with its own facility including pre-packing, ripening, sorting, repacking, storage and logistics.
In order to meet the emerging needs of health products in Hong Kong and around the Global.
We decide to target to high quality, high yielding and right ripeness level of fruits for different users and customers.
We are increasing investment in food technologies to make sure to keep the original quality and taste of our products and prolong the shelf life.
In January 2009, we have passed the IS022000 audit on top of HACCP accreditation, which means our production reaches the international food safety standard.
In 2019, Fresh-cut moved into our new building premises. We bring in mechanization to improve efficiency and competitiveness.
